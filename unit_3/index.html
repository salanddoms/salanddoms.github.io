Our Story.

Sal & Dom's Pastry Shop was established in 1956 by Salvatore Rapisarda and his son-in-law Domenick Cavallaro. Sal had been an established baker when he arrived from his native Sicily after WWII, while Dom had apprenticed at several top bakeries throughout New York City. Sal & Dom joined forces to create one of the most beloved bakeries in the Bronx.

Morning Treats

Turnovers, danish, donuts, muffins, and croissants.

Cookies

Assorted plain, chocolate dipped, or jelly filled butter cookies. Rainbow and pignoli cookies as well.

Bisccoti

Assorted nut, anisette toast, anginettes, vanilla, and seeded biscotti.

Pastries

Classics such as cannolis and sfogliatelle, as well as, french napoleons, eclairs, and cream puffs.

Cakes

Ready made cakes in various flavors available in our standard size. Larger, customized, or tiered cakes must be ordered in advance.

Pies

Standard flavors include apple, blueberry, and cherry. Pumpkin, sweet potato, and coconut custard are available in the fall.

Seasonal

Traditional holiday items such as cuccidati and struffoli, as well as sfingi and zeppole. These are available select times of the year.

Others

Cupcakes, cheesecake, coffee rings, and poundcake among other declicious treats.

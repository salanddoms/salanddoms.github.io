Our Story.

Sal & Dom's Pastry Shop was established in 1956 by Salvatore Rapisarda and his son-in-law Domenick Cavallaro. Sal had been an established baker when he arrived from his native Sicily after WWII, while Dom had apprenticed at several top bakeries throughout New York City. Sal & Dom joined forces to create one of the most beloved bakeries in the Bronx.
